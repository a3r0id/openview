OpenView Toolkit v1.1

Thanks for choosing OpenView for your geo-ip information needs!

I look forward to keeping this open-source and updated.

Usage:

Geo-IP: Simply click our Extension's icon in your browser to view insights about your current remote host.
To do a custom search, head over to the search module and enter a host then hit the Geo-IP button.

NMAP: Just click the NMAP button to run a quick NMAP scan on your current remote host.
To do a custom scan, head over to the search module and enter enter a hostname or IP in the input box then select the NMAP button.
Scans common ports and returns port-service plus rdns records and latency.*
[*] - NMAP is limited, the API allows 25 free scans per-day.
